Typography

Headers and Sizing

Header sizes are defined from h1 to h6. Additionally, Solid provides utility classes ranging from .text-1 to .text-6 for sizing blocks of text. Headers and text sizing classes have no margins or padding by default.

h1, .text-1 2.25rem, 36px

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

h2, .text-2 1.375rem, 22px

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

h3, .text-3 1.125rem, 18px

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

h4, .text-4 1rem, 16px

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

h5, .text-5 0.875rem, 16px

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company.

h6, .text-6 0.75rem, 12px

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company.

Slab Headers

Caponi Slab Semibold is BuzzFeed News' headline typeface (please avoid any other usage). It can be applied to h1 through h5 and .text-1 through .text-5 using .slab .

.slab

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

Emphasis

Use the following html tags and utility classes to add emphasis to blocks of text.

.regular

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

<p class="bold"><span class="regular">BuzzFeed is the</span>social news and entertainment company</p>

.strong, .bold

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

<p class="bold">BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company</p>

.em, .italic

BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company

<em>BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company</em>
<p class="italic">BuzzFeed is the social news and entertainment company</p>

.caps

Lists

Ordered and unordered lists require no classes by default. To remove all styling from either list, use the .list-unstyled class.

ol

  1. John
  2. Paul
  3. George
  4. Ringo

ul

  • John
  • Paul
  • George
  • Ringo

.list-unstyled

  • John
  • Paul
  • George
  • Ringo
<ol>

</ol>
